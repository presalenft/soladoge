ABOUT

Soladoge is an NFT based project borned with a very clear goal to achieve, create and develop utilities that we all knew were necessary for Web3, but that nobody dared to carry out due to the risk/benefit incurred. Our our goal is to meet those needs in our specific niche, Digital Art, applied to NFTs. It is not our intention to make the public believe that we are a non-profit organization, we are not, but we intend to benefit from a real service and for the public, with benefits for both of us, the developers and our holders.
